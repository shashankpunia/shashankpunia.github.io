Projects

AgroSquad

AgroSquad is a unit comprising of wirelessly communicating autonomous sub-systems that make use of image processing, smart embedded systems with sensors and algorithms specially designed for surveying and managing agricultural landscapes for optimizing crop production while ensuring efficient use of water, soil enhancement products and pesticides.

DriX

A voice-enabled intelligent driving-pattern recognition and analyzing system to enhance driving experience and security, while recording real-time driving behavior data from individualized systems for data extrapolation.

Deep Vision

Real time object recognition and caption generation using deep learning. The project was designed to help the visually impaired by building a portable device that generates real time captions of the surrounding.

Sewersway

A lower powered device based on LoRa, mounted on the surface of manholes to monitor harmful gas concentration and drainage levels in real-time. Multiple nodes create a mesh and communicate over LoRaWAN to alert municipal authorities (BBMP & BWSSB) about clogged manholes in a real-time map GUI containing data from all nodes.
